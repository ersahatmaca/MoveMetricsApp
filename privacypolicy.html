Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [17/09/2023]
1. Introduction

Welcome to Move Metrics, a fitness app designed for iOS. At Move Metrics, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or data from users of Move Metrics. However, with your explicit permission, we access and display data from Apple Health to provide you with a better way to track and visualize your health and fitness metrics.

3. Use of Information

We access data from Apple Health with your consent and display it within the Move Metrics app to offer you an enhanced and user-friendly health and fitness experience. We do not use your data for any other purpose, and we do not store your data on our servers.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Any data accessed from Apple Health is displayed within the Move Metrics app and is not stored or transmitted to our servers or third parties. Your health data remains secure on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Move Metrics may access data from Apple Health, but it does not integrate with any other third-party services, such as social media platforms or advertising networks. Therefore, your usage of the app is not linked to any external services.

6. Children's Privacy

Move Metrics is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the beginning of this policy. We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
